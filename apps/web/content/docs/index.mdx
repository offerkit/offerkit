Welcome to **open-voucherify** — a self-hostable promotion engine built around vouchers, gift cards, loyalty programs, referrals, and a flexible validation rules engine.

## What's here

- **Dashboard** at `/dashboard` — manage campaigns, customers, segments, vouchers.
- **REST API** at `/api/v1/...` — programmatic access for your storefront and back-office.
- **OpenAPI spec** at `/api/openapi.json` — typed clients are auto-generated.
- **MCP server** — agentic control over the API.

## Phase 1 status

Foundation only: auth, db schema, observability, worker queue, and an empty dashboard.
Real promotion features (campaigns, vouchers, validation, redemption) ship in Phase 2+.
